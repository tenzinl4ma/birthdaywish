For Nadia, on Her 18th

Candles burn, and cakes grow small,
Flowers fade, and balloons may fall.
But like this site which never fades,
Our friendship stands, it never shades.

Through time and change, both near and far,
I’ll cheer you on for who you are.
Happy 18th, Nadia — bright and true,
A lasting page, a friend for you.
